Hi, I’m Liza Cheung 👋🏻


I’m currently a UX Designer at BZun, a full-service agency that provides design and technology solutions to various brand enterprises. Clients include Nike, GUCCI, Reebok, Converse, Adidas, I.T etc.


Previously I was a Product Designer at Citrix.


As a designer, I have empathy for our users. I always put myself in their shoes, trying to figure out their needs and pain points before I jump into design. As a team player, I enjoy working with a group of people to solve complex problems through user-centered design. It’s challenging to build consensus among the stakeholders with different backgrounds. But if it’s a good design it’s worth the time to educate and convince people, articulate the rationale behind it and defend it. I believe that design makes the world a better place to work and live.


When I’m not sitting behind my computer, I can usually be found working out in the gym, watching Game of Thrones, or hanging out with my friends.


Always open to talk. 😉 ☕
