Hi there, I’m Tingting ZHANG. 😃


I’m currently a UX Designer at BAOZUN, a full-service tech company that provides e-Commerce solutions to enterprises from all over the globe. Our clients come from various industries, including fashion, beauty, food and beverage, automotive industry, etc. Products include SaaS (CMS, CRM, data platform, etc.), responsive website, mobile app, O2O tool.


Previously I was a Product Designer at Citrix, working on localized recruiting system and virtualization cloud service - SaaS product.


As a designer, I have empathy for our users. I always put myself in their shoes, trying to figure out their needs and pain points before I jump into design. As a team player, I enjoy working with a group of people to solve complex problems through user-centered design. I believe that design makes the world a better place to work and live.


When I’m not sitting behind my computer, I can usually be found working out in the gym, hiking, watching movies, or hanging out with my friends.


Always open to talk. 😉 ☕
