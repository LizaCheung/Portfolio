Hi there, I’m Tingting ZHANG. 😃


I’m currently a UX design lead at BAOZUN, a tech company that concentrates in e-Commerce and assists global enterprises go digital with full-service solutions ranging from e-shops, sales, operations, logistics, warehouse, etc. I’ve been working on both enterprise-faced and consumer-faced products, including SaaS (CMS, CRM, data platform, etc.), responsive website, mobile app, O2O tool and many more other innovative products.


Previously I was a Product Designer at Citrix, working on a localized recruiting system and virtualization cloud SaaS products.


As a designer with over 5+ years experience, in whatever business model, I’ve been used to adopt an empathetic mindset throughout the design process, bridging the gap between the business side and the user side. I interact with the clients to gain insights into their business and translate client requirements into viable user experiences while taking into considerations the development constraints and cost management inside the organization and always standing by the users' side. Collaborating and co-working with people from distinct backgrounds, I adopt a mix of methodologies of CCD (conversion-centered design) and UCD (user-centered design) throughout the process to solve complex problems. There’re always a lot of stories to tell behind each project from research, ideation, design, implementation to iteration and I’d be very glad to share with you the moments I make crucial decisions and trade-offs under agile environments. I feel very grateful that for the past few years until now I’ve been surrounded by a plethora of talented people who all share a passion for innovative breakthrough and I’m proud of my team and the hard work we make to transfer the world into a better place to work and live.


When I’m not sitting behind my computer, I can usually be found working out, hiking, jogging, watching movies, or hanging out with my friends.


Always open to talk. 😉 ☕
